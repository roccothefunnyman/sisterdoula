On tending 01

Every family deserves to be tended to.

Tending is small, attentive work. Questions answered before they get asked. A hand on a shoulder when the room gets loud. The right thing brought before anyone has to ask for it.

You don't have to know what you want. You don't have to have a plan. You have to be willing to be tended to, and we will figure the rest out together.

The circle 02

Your care team, on your terms.

What we tend to, together, before and through your birth.

  • 01 Your village The people, the rhythm, the room.
  • 02 Your vision What you want this birth to feel like, before anyone else gets a vote.
  • 03 Your choices There are more options than the pamphlet shows. We go through them.
  • 04 Your partner So they can show up ready and supported, not stranded in the corner.
  • 05 Your questions All of them. The first consult is free.
  • 06 Your care team The circle around your birth: providers, family, friends, on your terms.
About 03

Holding space for all kinds of birth.

I'm Melissa Cunningham. I trained as a birth doula and I work in Los Angeles and the surrounding county.

I work with first-time parents, second-time parents, partners, queer families, and anyone whose birth doesn't fit a brochure. I don't have a script. We figure out what your kind of birth looks like, and then I help you get there.
