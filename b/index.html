№ 01 · on tending

Every family deserves to be tended to.

A birth doula's practice is small, attentive work. Questions answered before they get asked. A hand on a shoulder when the room gets loud. The right thing brought before anyone has to ask for it.

№ 02 · the circle

Holding space for all kinds of birth.

What we tend to, together, before and through your birth.

Your village
The people, the rhythm, the room.
Your vision
What you want this birth to feel like, before anyone else gets a vote.
Your choices
There are more options than the pamphlet shows. We go through them.
Your birth partner
So they can show up ready and supported, not stranded in the corner.
Your questions

№ 03 · about

A short note from Melissa.

I'm Melissa Cunningham. I trained as a birth doula and I work in Los Angeles and the surrounding county.

I work with first-time parents, second-time parents, partners, queer families, and anyone whose birth doesn't fit a brochure. I don't have a script. We figure out what your kind of birth looks like, and then I help you get there.

Melissa
