Three directions.

Three working drafts of the tending's site, none of which look like the AI default. Pick one and we'll build it out.

  1. A

    Editorial broadsheet

    Treats the site like a small print magazine: masthead, numbered dispatches, italic headlines, hairline rules. Source Serif body at a generous size on cream. No cards, no shadows, no rounded corners.

  2. B

    Letterpress & botanical

    Leans into the Instagram aesthetic and pushes harder. Italiana display, Crimson Pro body, full-bleed color blocks from her palette switching per section, hand-drawn line ornaments instead of icons.

    view →
  3. C

    Quiet brutalism

    Single color block, very large left-aligned type set tight, Fraunces with optical-size shifts doing the work, mono for metadata. No hero button. Links are inline text. Closer to an architect's portfolio than a wellness site.
